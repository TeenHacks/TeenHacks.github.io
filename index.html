What is a hackathon?

Hacking is not about breaking into your school computers. Hackathons are a place where students come together to work on fun projects using technology. It's a great time to hangout with friends, work on something you care about, and have food paid for. Check out this blog post written by a hackathon veteran.

What will I do at Teenhacks?

TeenHacks is a 24 hour overnight event where teams with the guidance of mentors learn to turn their ideas into working prototypes. Mentors will help everyone, from absolute beginner to grizzled veteran. It's an incredible learning experience. Attend one and you won't regret it.

What if I don't know how to code or build robots or do any of that?

TeenHacks is for beginners and experts alike. As long as you want to build something (whether you know how to or not), you can come. A main focus of this event is to introduce beginners. Mentors will be available throughout the event to get you started.

Do I need to have a team?

Nope. Just come to the event and we will find you a team. We'll be a holding a team making session before the hacking begins. You can also find teammates on Facebook beforehand. Working alone is fine too. Teams can have at most 4 people.

Who can attend?

Anyone currently enrolled in high school who wants to learn or improve their skills. Make this event more awesome by inviting your friends. Let's make TeenHacks the most epic HS hackathon ever.

What should I bring?

All your friends, computer (laptop or desktop), school ID, phone, toiletries, sleeping bag (optional), and anything else you might need.

What can I make?

Just about anything. You could make your first website, your first iPhone app, your first Android app, your first robot. The possibilities are endless. Whatever it is, mentors will get you started.

When is this taking place?

For now, just leave us your email and we'll let you know when we have more details.

How much does this cost?

This event is completely free. Meals and snacks are provided the entire event. All you need to worry about is learning and building. We'll take care of the rest.

Can I work on an existing project?

All work has to be done at the event.
