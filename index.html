What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a weekend event where teams create awesome projects and then demo them for prizes and bragging rights. It's also a chance to meet other talented peers and to connect with high profile tech companies. Attend one and you won't regret it.

What if I don't know how to code?

TeenHacks is for beginners and experts alike. If you don't know how to code, it's absolutely no problem. Mentors will be available throughout the event to help you get started.

Do I need to have a team?

Nope! Just come to the event and you can find a team there. We will be a holding a team making session before the coding begins. You can also find teammates on Facebook beforehand. Working alone is perfectly fine too.

Who can attend?

Anyone currently enrolled in high school. Also, you don't have to be a coder. For example, you could be a designer on a team. As long as you can contribute to making something cool, you can attend.

What should I bring?

All your friends, laptop and charger, school ID, phone, toiletries, and maybe an idea or two.

What can I make?

Just about anything. You could make your first website, your first iOS app, your first Android app. The possibilities are endless. Whatever you can think of, mentors can help you get started.

When is this taking place?

For now, just leave us your email and we'll let you know when we have more details. The more people that signup, the awesomer this event will be. So tell your friends! Let's make TeenHacks the biggest and baddest HS hackathon the world has ever seen.

How much does this cost?

This event is completely free. Food and snacks are provided. All you need to worry about is building. We'll take care of the rest.

Can I work on an existing project?

Sorry, no. All work has to be done at the event.
